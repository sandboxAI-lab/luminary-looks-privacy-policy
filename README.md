Privacy Policy for Luminary Looks

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information Collection and Use
Luminary Looks is designed to respect your privacy. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number to use the core features of the app.

2. Third-Party Services and Affiliate Links
To support the development of the app, we participate in affiliate marketing programs. The app contains links to external websites and third-party products.
When you click on these links or make a purchase, third-party affiliate networks may use cookies, tracking pixels, or your device’s IP address to track referrals and process commissions. We do not have access to or control over the data collected by these third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

3. Log Data and Analytics
If the app crashes or encounters an error, Apple may collect crash logs (if you have opted into sharing this data with developers). This data is completely anonymous and is only used to help identify and fix bugs to improve the app's performance.

4. Security
We value your trust and use commercially acceptable means to protect any data associated with the app. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
